NVIDIA GTC 2026OmniverseAlpamayo
Campus Inspection & Ackermann Digital Twin 校園巡檢與阿克曼小車數位孿生

This project represents the pinnacle of my industrial-academic collaboration. Exhibited at the MSI DGX Spark booth at NVIDIA GTC 2026 in California, we developed a comprehensive autonomous campus inspection system. Working closely with MSI Senior Manager Hung Shih-Che and RD Lai Yen-Cheng, I utilized NVIDIA Alpamayo and Digital Twin technology to simulate and deploy the Ackermann steering car. The project's innovation and execution were recognized with a personal signature from NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang. 這是我產學合作的旗艦專案，並成功於 美國加州 NVIDIA GTC 2026 微星 DGX Spark 攤位 展出。我與微星創新前瞻中心資深經理洪士哲及 RD 賴彥成緊密合作，利用 NVIDIA Alpamayo 與數位孿生技術，打造出完整的校園自主巡檢與阿克曼轉向小車系統。該專案的技術落地能力，更獲得了 NVIDIA 執行長 黃仁勳 的親筆簽名肯定。

MGX ServerNemoClawLLM Integration
AI Infrastructure & NemoClaw Deployment 高階 AI 基礎設施與 NemoClaw 整合

Transitioning from pure robotics to infrastructure, I took charge of the MGX Server environment setup and system upgrades. A significant challenge in Omniverse is the steep learning curve for new developers. To solve this, I successfully deployed NemoClaw on the DGX Spark. By integrating Large Language Models (LLM), we created an intuitive interface that dramatically lowers the technical barrier for beginners entering the Omniverse ecosystem. 從純機器人控制跨足底層基礎設施，我負責了 MGX Server 的環境建置與系統升級。為了解決新手開發者難以快速上手 Omniverse 的痛點，我成功在 DGX Spark 上部署了 NemoClaw。透過整合大型語言模型 (LLM)，我們打造了能用自然語言輔助開發的工作流，大幅降低了進入數位孿生生態系的技術門檻。

Sensor FusionLinux/MySQLUnity
Hardware-in-the-Loop & Sensor Fusion 實體高爾夫球車控制與多感測器融合

Building a strong foundation in low-level hardware control, I engineered the autonomous navigation system for a full-scale Golf Cart. This involved deep Linux environment configuration, MySQL for telemetry data management, and Unity for preliminary visualization. I successfully integrated raw sensor data—including LiDAR, IMU, and Vision Cameras—into the ROS2 framework, enabling robust perception for Ackermann steering platforms. 為了打下扎實的底層硬體控制基礎，我投入了實體高爾夫球車的自動化控制開發。這涵蓋了 Linux 開發環境的建置、使用 MySQL 管理遙測數據，以及利用 Unity 進行初步視覺化。我成功將 LiDAR、IMU 與視覺相機 等底層感測器訊號接入 ROS2 架構中，為阿克曼轉向平台建立了可靠的環境感知能力。
